Action

With an aim to uplift the Women hold i.e currently 26% of tech-related roles in the global workforce, according to Gartner Research, AWS Girls’ Tech Day was initiated to educate, inspire, and empower girls and young women aged 8 to 24 to pursue careers in technology. It enabled future female builders to Think Big and ignite their imaginations through STEM. Igniting the vision of Learning by Doing, STEMpedia crafted a customized AWS theme project that opened doors for volunteers attending sessions to provide support at all 4 centres in USA, India, Indonesia and UK.

Our Upcoming Projects:

Unveiling New Projects for Boundless Growth and Learning!

Revival of 100+ Tinkering Labs in Assam & Northeast

The upcoming project aims to revive the existing Tinkering Labs in Assam and the Northeast region. These labs will be equipped with advanced tools, equipment, and resources to promote innovation and practical learning among students. The project will focus on enhancing the infrastructure, training teachers, and providing necessary support to ensure the effective functioning of the Tinkering Labs.

Skill Training & Job Placement of 200+ Candidates

This project focuses on providing skill training and job placement opportunities to over 200 candidates. The training programs will be designed to develop relevant skills in line with industry requirements, empowering individuals with employable skills. The project will also facilitate job placement assistance and linkages with potential employers, increasing the employment prospects of the trained candidates.

Innovation Lab Setup, Pilot Setup in KV Schools

Innovation Labs will be set up in Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV) schools as part of this project. These labs will serve as dedicated spaces for fostering creativity, problem-solving, and innovation among students. The labs will be equipped with cutting-edge technology and resources to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation. Additionally, pilot setups will be initiated to test and refine innovative teaching and learning methodologies within the KV school system.

Setup of Maker Space, Experimental Centre for Student Coaching & Workshops

The upcoming project aims to establish Maker Spaces and Experimental Centers in educational institutions. These spaces will provide students with opportunities to engage in practical projects, hands-on experiments, and workshops related to various disciplines. The focus will be on promoting creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary learning. The Maker Spaces and Experimental Centers will serve as hubs for student coaching, mentoring, and collaborative problem-solving activities
