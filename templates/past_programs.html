OBJECTIVE

  • Empower teachers to establish Tinkering Labs in their respective schools
  • Foster scientific temperament and 21st-century skills among students
  • Integrate activities into the curriculum for experiential learning, nurturing creativity, critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration.
  • Experience 'learning-by-doing' and think 'out-of-box'

ACTION

In 2021, the implementation of Tinkering Labs was initiated in 102 schools in Assam. With continued efforts, the initiative expanded to 51 new schools in 2022 and in 2023, Tinkering Lab implementation further expanded to 69 schools.

3 teachers on average from each school attended a 5-day residential training program at IIT Guwahati. Furthermore, Tinkering lab kits were delivered and installed in all schools, and all nominated students underwent physical training in their respective schools

IMPACT

{{program.impact}} Tinkering Lab is a creative and hands-on learning space where individuals can explore, experiment, and invent using a wide range of tools, materials, and technologies. Through hands-on activities and projects, Tinkering Labs encourage innovation, curiosity, and a deep understanding of concepts across various disciplines
